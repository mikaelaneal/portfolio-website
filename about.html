About Me

My Story

Hi, I'm Mikaela. I'm a UX designer with a background in academic research, located in Houston. I am passionate about studying human behaviors and attitudes and designing engaging and intuitive experiences that suit the wants and needs of my users.
I hold a Bachelors of Science in biomedical science and a specialization in cognitive studies that prepared me to pursue academic neuroscience research after graduation. This experience allowed me to develop the ability to design research questions relevant to the needs of the lab, while also helping me gain an eye for effectively presenting results to various audiences. In this role, I managed multiple projects without losing sight of the primary goals of my research, allowing me to learn how to balance the needs of my projects with the comprehensive needs of the lab. This skill transfers to my work in UX design--I am able to strategically balance user needs with business needs, allowing me to create practical products that provide an engaging and intuitive experience for my users.
I have experience in completing UX design projects from the initial research phase to the development of high-fidelity prototypes. I pride myself on my ability to merge strategical thinking with user-centric focus to develop UX projects that suit both my clients and their users.
